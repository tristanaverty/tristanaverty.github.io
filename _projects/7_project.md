**Topic** : MUSIT is an international and interdisciplinary project that transforms heterogeneous tracking sensor data into complete, semantically enriched trajectories, opening new perspectives in mobility monitoring and establishing a dynamic network of collaborations among academia, industry, and innovators.

**Funding** : MUSIT has received funding from the European Union's HORIZON-MSCA-2023-SE-01 program under grant agreement No 101182585.
